Completing partially entered words

You can view a list of possible completions for a partially entered word.

To complete a partially entered word:

Position the insertion point at the end of a partial word.

Choose Edit > Complete (or press Option-Esc), then select a word from the list.

If you try a partial word (such as “ret”), you will see options such as “retire,” “return,” or “retrieve.” If you try a complete word (such as “return”), the list of options will include more specific words, such as “returning” and “returned.”
