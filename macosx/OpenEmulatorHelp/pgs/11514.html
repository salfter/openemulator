Checking the spelling and grammar of an entire document

You can check the spelling for the entire document or a single word.

To check the spelling of a document:

Choose Edit > Spelling and Grammar > Show Spelling and Grammar.

■

To change the language, choose it from the pop-up menu.

■

To check your document’s grammar along with its spelling, select the “Check Grammar” checkbox.

Click Find Next.

If TextEdit finds a misspelled word or grammar error, do one of the following:

■

If one of the alternatives is correct, select it, and then click Change.

■

If none of the alternatives is correct, retype the expression, and then click Change.

■

If you do not want TextEdit to correct this expression in this document, click Ignore.

Text Edit won’t correct this expression again in the document, but TextEdit will correct it in other documents.

■

If you don’t want TextEdit to correct this expression in this and other documents, click Learn.

■

To continue checking without making any changes, click Find Next.
