Using smart quotes

TextEdit can automatically substitute curly (or “smart”) quotation marks for straight quotation marks as you type.

Curly quotation marks are commonly used in books and make your document look more professional.

To use smart quotes:

■

To use smart quotes in the current document, choose Edit > Substitutions > Smart Quotes.

■

To use smart quotes in all new documents, choose Text Edit > Preferences, and select “Smart quotes.”

You can enter straight quotation marks when “Smart quotes” is selected. To enter a straight single quotation mark, press Control-’ (apostrophe). To enter a straight double quotation mark, press Control-Shift-’.
