Creating a list of items

You can use bullets or numbers to distinguish items in a list.

To create a list:

Type your list, pressing Return at the end of each list item.

Select the list, then choose a bullet or number from the Lists pop-up menu. To customize a bullet or number, choose Other.

You can add more lines to your list by placing the insertion point at the end of a list item and pressing Return. To finish the list, press Return twice after the last list item.
