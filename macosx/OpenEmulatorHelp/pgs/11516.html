Checking spelling and grammar as you type

You can check for misspelled words or incorrect grammar as you type. As TextEdit finds errors, it underlines misspelled words in red and grammar errors in green.

To check for misspelled words as you type, do one of the following:

■

To check in the current document, choose Edit > “Spelling and Grammar” > Check Spelling While Typing. To check for grammar errors as well, choose Edit > “Spelling and Grammar” > Check Grammar With Spelling.

■

To check in new documents, choose TextEdit > Preferences, click New Document, and select “Check spelling as you type.” To check for grammar errors as well, select “Check grammar with spelling.”

To correct or ignore an error, hold down the Control key as you click it. A contextual menu appears.

■

To correct the error, choose one of the alternatives.

■

If you do not want TextEdit to correct this expression in this document, choose Ignore Spelling.

Text Edit won’t correct this expression again in this document but will correct it in other documents.

■

If you don’t want TextEdit to correct this expression in this and other documents, choose Learn Spelling.

To change the dictionary for this document, choose Edit > “Spelling and Grammar” > “Show Spelling and Grammar,” and then choose another dictionary from the pop-up menu.
