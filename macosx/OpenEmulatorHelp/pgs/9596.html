Checking spelling and grammar

You can check the spelling of text in your TextEdit documents. You can check the spelling for the entire document or a single word, or have TextEdit check your spelling as you type.

To check spelling and grammar:

■

To check the spelling and grammar of a document, choose Edit > “Spelling and Grammar” > “Show Spelling and Grammar.”

Checking the spelling and grammar of an entire document
■

To check the spelling of a single word, select it, and then choose Edit > “Spelling and Grammar” > “Show Spelling and Grammar.”

■

To check for misspelled words as you type, choose Edit > “Spelling and Grammar” > Check Spelling While Typing. To check for grammar errors as well, choose Edit > “Spelling and Grammar” > Check Grammar With Spelling.
