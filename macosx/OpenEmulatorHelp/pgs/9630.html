Adjusting text alignment

You can adjust text alignment for selected paragraphs.

To adjust text alignment:

Click in a paragraph or select a group of paragraphs you want to adjust.

Choose Format > Text and choose an alignment (left, center, justified, or right).

To change the alignment, make a different selection.

If you prefer to write from right to left instead of left to right, choose Format > Text > Writing Direction > “Right to Left.”
