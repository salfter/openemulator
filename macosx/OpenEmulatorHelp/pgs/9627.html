Identifying your documents using properties

To make your documents easy to find and identify, you can use document properties. You can add information such as an author’s name, comments, keywords, and more.

To specify the properties for new documents:

Choose TextEdit > Preferences.

Click New Document.

Type the information you want in the Properties section.

To change the properties for a document, choose File > Show Properties.

You can search your files’ properties with Spotlight.
