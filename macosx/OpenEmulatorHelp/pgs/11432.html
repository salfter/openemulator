Ruler icons for tab stops, paragraph indentation, and margins

There are four kinds of tab stops and two types of margins, each indicated by a differently shaped icon.

Here are the icons for tab stops, paragraph indentation, and margins:

■

A right-facing triangle is a tab stop that left-justifies text.

■

A diamond is a tab stop that centers text.

■

A left-facing triangle is a tab stop that right-justifies text.

■

A circle is a tab stop that justifies numbers around a decimal point.

■

A downward-facing triangle is a page margin.

■

A horizontal bar is the indentation for the first line of a paragraph.
